Quality After-School Programs

With your support, United Way works to improve after-school programs that keep kids out of trouble and on the right track.

200,000 Students Are Left
Unsupervised After School

Between 3 p.m. and 6 p.m. are peak hours for risky behavior. We help after-school centers offer mentoring and counseling to support kids who may be failing classes, not getting along with their parents or peers, or are going through other hard times.

The Situation

Students without positive adult relationships often struggle keeping up in class, managing their time, or simply making it to school.

Our Goal

Ensure 115,000 students have access to quality after-school programs.

Our Impact

What the power of after-school support can do for at-risk youth.

90%

of students in these programs showed a positive relationship with an adult—indicating academic success.

304,724

mentoring hours have gone toward helping kids succeed.
